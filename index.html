Hello! I'm the founder of PlumeriaEnglish and a native English speaker from New York City, dedicated to helping international students achieve their language goals with confidence and clarity.

I hold a BA in English from Queens College, City University of New York, and a Master of Education (MEd) in Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages from the University of Glasgow, Scotland.
